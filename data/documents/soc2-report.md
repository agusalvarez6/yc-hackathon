# Lyra AI — SOC 2 Type II Report

This SOC 2 Type II report covers Lyra AI's controls over the Security, Availability, and Confidentiality Trust Services Criteria for the audit period of January 1, 2025 through December 31, 2025. The audit was conducted by Schellman & Co., an independent licensed CPA firm, and the report was issued February 18, 2026. Marcus Rivera, Head of Security and Compliance, served as the Lyra control owner.

The auditors tested 112 controls across access management, change management, logical and physical security, system operations, vendor management, and incident response. No exceptions were noted in the Security or Confidentiality categories. One observation was logged in Availability regarding monitoring coverage of a sandbox region; remediation completed in Q4 2025 and re-tested clean.

Customer-facing artifacts include the full audit report, the bridge letter for the gap between the audit period and the current date, the management assertion, and a SIG Lite questionnaire prepopulated from the audit. Lyra refreshes the SOC 2 Type II audit annually and intends to add the Privacy criterion in the 2026 audit cycle.
