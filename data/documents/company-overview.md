# Lyra AI — Company Overview 2026

Lyra AI is an enterprise AI workflow automation company founded in 2022. We build production-grade copilots for regulated industries — banking, insurance, healthcare, and large-scale customer operations — by combining structured retrieval, tool-using agents, and human-in-the-loop review queues.

Our team of 38 employees is led by founder and CEO Maya Chen, who sets executive narrative, pricing approval, and overall strategy. Headquarters are in San Francisco with delivery hubs in Austin and Toronto. We currently serve 14 enterprise customers across financial services and healthcare, with average contract values above one million dollars per year.

The Lyra platform is deployed in customer-managed VPCs or on dedicated SaaS tenants. Every deployment ships with audit logging, role-based access control, and a configurable policy engine that lets compliance teams define which actions a copilot may take autonomously versus which require human approval. Every workflow is versioned so regulators can replay exactly what an automated system did, and why, six months after the fact.
