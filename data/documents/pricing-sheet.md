# Lyra AI — Pricing Sheet 2026

Lyra AI offers three enterprise tiers. Pricing is owned by Sarah Goldberg, Finance and Operations, with executive approval required from Maya Chen on terms outside the published bands.

The Growth tier starts at $180,000 per year and includes up to 25 named seats, a single production workspace, standard SSO, business-hours support, and 99.5 percent availability SLA. The Enterprise tier starts at $480,000 per year and adds unlimited seats with named licensing, multi-workspace deployment, customer-managed encryption keys, 24x7 support, 99.9 percent availability SLA, and dedicated solutions architect time. The Enterprise Plus tier starts at $1.2M per year, is reserved for regulated industries, and adds private VPC deployment, dedicated tenant inference, custom data residency, and a named compliance liaison.

All tiers can be priced as per-seat, per-workflow, or hybrid per-seat-plus-outcome bonus. Multi-year contracts qualify for 8 percent annual escalation discounts. Payment terms are net 30 by default; net 45 and net 60 are available on multi-year deals over $500,000. Pricing approval, rebates, and any deviation from the standard bands route through Sarah Goldberg.
